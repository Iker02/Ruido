Why We’re Here

We believe in aumenting human intelligence, not replacing it.

With good data and the right technology, people and institutions today can still solve hard problems and change the world for the better.

In 2015, after we looked at the available technology, we saw products that were too rigid to handle novel problems, and custom systems that took too long to deploy and required too many resources to maintain and improve.

We also witnessed agencies that failed against mission-critical workloads, data breaches from off-the-shelf tooling, and underserved communities left behind.

We saw a need for a different kind of technology, where impact would be measured not by ease of adoption, but by actual outcomes. That’s why we started Palantir.
